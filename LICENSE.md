This software and associated mathematical formulations implement the **Convergent Time Theory (CTT)** solution to the 3D Navier-Stokes existence and smoothness problem, including:

- Theorem 4.2 (Temporal Energy Decay)
- Theorem 4.3 (Vorticity Bound)
- The constant α = 0.0302011 as applied to fluid dynamics
- All spectral solver implementations derived from CTT principles
